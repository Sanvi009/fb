Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This policy explains what information Runbook collects when you visit this site and how it's used.

Information we collect

Like most websites, we use standard analytics tools to understand which guides are useful, which rely on data such as pages visited, approximate location (city/country level, derived from IP address), device and browser type, and referring site. We do not collect names, email addresses, or other personal information unless you voluntarily provide it, for example through our contact form.

Cookies and advertising

This site may display advertising served by third-party providers, including Google. These providers may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to this site and/or other sites on the Internet.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings, or opt out of some third-party vendor use of cookies via the Digital Advertising Alliance opt-out page.

Third-party links

Our guides sometimes link to external tools, documentation, or software mentioned in a fix. We aren't responsible for the privacy practices of those external sites, and we recommend reviewing their own policies.

Data retention

Analytics data is retained only as long as necessary to understand site performance and is not sold to third parties.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent through our contact page.
